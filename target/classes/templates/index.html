Welcome to Omegron!

Omegron a web-based application that facilitates the work of agribusiness administration. Its aim is to contain all the necessary information for reports and contracts in one easily and quickly accessible place. In addition, it has the most important functions for a farmer by dynamically providing up-to-date information on weather, inventory and machinery.
